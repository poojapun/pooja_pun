Budget Calculator

Click here to try the budget calculator! Click here for code

Budget calculator takes your monthly income and expenses and calculates your savings for the month. It also displays a pie-chart for you so you can better visualize how your money is being spent.

I have used Google charts to create the pie-chart. This is a dynamic web app, so you can update the values of your expenses and monthly income, and the app will dynamically update the chart as well as your monthly savings. You can add as many expenses as you like using the Add Expense button.

Hope you make the most out of the budget calculator!
