Proposal for a Full Stack App

My teammate: Bipin Pachhai

Bipin is a sophmore CS student. He is very into web dev. I think he and I will be able to achieve our goals for this app.

We are proposing to develop a messaging application with additional functionalities like to-do list, bill splitting which will make it easier for people to chat with their friends and plan their next trip without switching apps. We think our application will be most useful for roommates to plan their finances and keep track of the spending. The main features of our application will be group messaging, to-do list, expenditure tracking, summary of the expenditure and if possible, we want to integrate banking system in our application.

For the frontend, we will have a chat window, a to-do list window and an expenditure tracking window. We plan to add sign in / sign out features to our application. We plan on using React for the frontend portion of our application. For the backend, we will need store all the text messages, the status of to-do list and the expenditure tracking. As for the database, we are planning on using MongoDB. We need to store the to-do list and expenditure tracker with the most up-to date data.
