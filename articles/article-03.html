Food Guessing Game

Click here to try the food guessing game! Click here for code

How many food do you know? Show off your knowledge!

I use fetch to get food images from Foodish API. Then, I build four options including the correct answer and shuffle the options. After you click on the submit button, it let's you know whether you have provied the correct answer or not. If you didn't get the first time, you can always play again by clicking on the play again button.
